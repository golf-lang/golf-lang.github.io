P num
Purpose: Outputs a number.

 p-num <number>  [ new-line ]
Copied!

p-num outputs a number given by <number> variable.

If "new-line" clause is used, then a new line ("\n") is output after <number>.
Examples
To output a number to a client:
 set-number x = 100
 p-num x
Copied!
